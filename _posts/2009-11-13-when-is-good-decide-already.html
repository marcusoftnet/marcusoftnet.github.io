I been involved in many meeting booking activities during the last month. And it’s always the same – you send dates back and forth and finally decide.

If you have read Lean Software Development by the Poppendiecks you know about Set-based problem solving.

The WhenIsGood-website uses this approach to decide on an available meeting time, in a very simple and low-impact fashion. Look for yourself at their introduction video.
