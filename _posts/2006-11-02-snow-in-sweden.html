Today, and most of all yestday was the first snowstorm of the winter in the Stockholm-area.


Every, i'm saying every, year we miss this. First the trains is stopped by falling leafs and then, what do you know, snow? Here in Sweden? How can it happen?


That never fails. Yesterday was worse than ever (litterally thousands of people not being able to get home, all busses in the Stockholm area stopped etc) but then again we knew about this storm for about 2 weeks.


Maybe if the busses have had winter tires on, or all other vehicles for that matter (people have left their cars in the middle of the road tonight).


Anyway, this gets us each year and i am not expecting any co-workers until 10 o'clock today.
